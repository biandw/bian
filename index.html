⏰ Past Tense

Pelajari cara berbicara tentang kejadian di masa lalu dalam bahasa Inggris dengan mudah dan menyenangkan!

📅
Simple Past Tense
Kejadian yang sudah selesai di masa lalu
Rumus Positif
Subject + Verb 2 (past form)
Rumus Negatif
Subject + did not (didn't) + Verb 1
Rumus Tanya
Did + Subject + Verb 1?
  • She visited Paris last summer.
    Dia mengunjungi Paris musim panas lalu.
  • They didn't go to the party.
    Mereka tidak pergi ke pesta.
  • Did you eat breakfast this morning?
    Apakah kamu sarapan pagi ini?
  • I studied English yesterday.
    Saya belajar bahasa Inggris kemarin.
💡 Kata Kunci Waktu
yesterday, last week/month/year, ago, in 2020, this morning, just now

Harus dihafal karena tidak mengikuti aturan + -ed

✏️
Kata Kerja Beraturan (Regular Verbs)
Cukup tambahkan -ed atau -d di akhir kata
Verb 1 (Now) Verb 2 (Past) Arti
walk walked berjalan
study studied belajar
play played bermain
cook cooked memasak
watch watched menonton
call called menelepon
listen listened mendengarkan
visit visited mengunjungi
love loved mencintai
help helped membantu
💡 Aturan Penulisan
• Umumnya: + -ed (walk → walked)
• Berakhiran -e: + -d (love → loved)
• Berakhiran konsonan + y: ganti jadi -ied (study → studied)
• Konsonan tunggal setelah vokal: ganda + -ed (stop → stopped)
⏳
Past Continuous Tense
Kejadian yang sedang berlangsung di masa lalu
Rumus
Subject + was/were + Verb-ing
  • I was sleeping when the phone rang.
    Saya sedang tidur ketika telepon berdering.
  • They were watching TV all evening.
    Mereka sedang menonton TV sepanjang malam.
  • She was cooking dinner at 7 PM.
    Dia sedang memasak makan malam pada pukul 7.
💡 was vs were
was → I, he, she, it  |  were → you, we, they
⌛
Past Perfect Tense
Kejadian yang terjadi sebelum kejadian lain di masa lalu
Rumus
Subject + had + Verb 3 (past participle)
  • She had already eaten when he arrived.
    Dia sudah makan ketika dia tiba.
  • I had never seen snow before I went to Europe.
    Saya belum pernah melihat salju sebelum pergi ke Eropa.
  • They had finished the project before the deadline.
    Mereka telah menyelesaikan proyek sebelum batas waktu.
